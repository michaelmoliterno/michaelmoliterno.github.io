Predicting the Risk of Type 2 Diabetes

I recently revistied an old Kaggle competition that challenged entrants to use electronic health records (EHR) from Practice Fusion's database to identify patients diagnosed with Type 2 Diabetes. Given a set of ~10,000 electronic health records, I developed a set of classifiers for Type 2 Diabetes (an article on this analysis will be posted sortly). While my models were not as good as the winning models, I was happy with my results; I think would have placed in about the middle of the leaderboard if I has entered when the competetion was active.
