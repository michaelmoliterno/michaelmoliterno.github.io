Introduction

NYC Open Data (https://nycopendata.socrata.com) is a very cool initiatice that makes the wealth of public data generated by various New York City agencies and other City organizations available for public use. One of these data sets is MTA (Subway) Turnstile data, which details entrances and exits for every turnstile in the NYC Subway system. For this project, I ingested the raw text files, cleaned the data, and performed exporatory analysis on the data.
